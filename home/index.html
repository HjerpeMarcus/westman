Meet Gustaf Westman

Gustaf Westman, established in 2020 by the architect-turned-designer, is a Stockholm-based design studio. The namesake label presents objects including made-to-order furniture, custom design pieces, ceramics and glassware.

Restaurant and winebar

Vingården offers a serene escape with its selection of biodynamic wines, thoughtfully curated by GEIST and set amidst the captivating design by Gustaf Westman. Nestled within the vibrant ambiance of Trädgården in Stockholm, it provides a delightful haven where patrons can indulge in exquisite cuisine paired with exceptional wines, surrounded by the distinctive charm of Westman's creations.
